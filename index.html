Circuit Board Repair

We fix the tiny, complex components on your motherboards that typical repair shops can't handle.

  • ↗︎ Micro-Soldering & PCB Rework and Repair Removing and replacing damaged microchips to save you from buying a whole new board.
  • ↗︎ Trace & Pad Reconstruction Rebuilding severed electrical pathways and repairing physically cracked or damaged circuit boards.
  • ↗︎ Liquid Damage CleanupSafely removing corrosion and repairing the micro-components damaged by liquid exposure

Secure Data Recovery

Retrieving your important files, photos, and business documents from broken or completely dead devices.

  • ↗︎ Dead Device Extraction Getting your data back from devices that won't turn on or charge.
  • ↗︎ Direct Memory Reading Physically removing the memory chips to read your files when the rest of the device is destroyed.
  • ↗︎ Temporary Board Repair Fixing the electronics just enough to safely copy your files to a secure drive.

Everyday Electronics

Professional, reliable fixes for the devices you rely on daily, making them work perfectly again.

  • ↗︎ Controller Stick Drift Fix Replacing the worn-out internal parts of your joysticks so you have perfect control.
  • ↗︎ Broken Port Replacement Fixing loose or completely broken charging ports, HDMI ports, and USB connections.
  • ↗︎ Screen & Battery Swaps Installing high-quality replacement screens and fresh batteries to bring devices back to life.
